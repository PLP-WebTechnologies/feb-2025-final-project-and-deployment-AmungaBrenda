Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

+

We offer a 30-day return policy on all unused items in their original packaging. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact us to initiate a return.

How long does shipping take?

+

Standard shipping within the US takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days, depending on the destination.

Are your products safe for babies with allergies?

+

All of our products are free from common allergens like latex and BPA. Our organic cotton products are ideal for babies with sensitive skin. However, if your child has specific allergies, please contact us directly to discuss whether a particular product is suitable.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

+

Yes! We offer eco-friendly gift wrapping for $5 per item. You can add this option at checkout and include a personalized message for the recipient.

About Tiny Treasures

Our story, mission, and commitment to quality

Our Story

Tiny Treasures began with a simple mission: to create baby products that are safe, sustainable, and beautiful.

When our founder, Brenda, became a mother in 2016, she was overwhelmed by the number of baby products on the market that contained harmful chemicals, were made from non-sustainable materials, or were simply designed to be disposable.

Drawing on her background in sustainable product design, Brenda began creating her own baby essentials using natural, eco-friendly materials. Friends and family were soon asking where they could buy these thoughtfully designed products, and Tiny Treasures was born.

Today, we work with a small team of artisans and ethical manufacturers to bring you baby products that are gentle on your child and on our planet.

Our Values

Safety First

Every product we sell undergoes rigorous safety testing and exceeds industry standards. We believe that what touches your baby's skin and goes into their mouth should be absolutely safe.

Eco-Friendly

We're committed to minimizing our environmental footprint by using organic, renewable, or recycled materials whenever possible, and designing products that last.

Ethical Production

We partner only with manufacturers who provide fair wages and safe working conditions. Many of our products are handcrafted by artisans who are paid fairly for their skills.

Quality Design

We believe that baby products should be beautiful as well as functional. Our designs are thoughtfully created to grow with your child and stand the test of time.
